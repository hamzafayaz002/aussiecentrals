Baffle Filters

Engineered for efficiency and durability, baffle filters work by forcing airflow to rapidly shift direction, effectively trapping grease particles and reducing the risk of fire.

Key Benefits
  • Built for long-lasting use with heavy-duty construction
  • Captures grease efficiently, reducing cleaning frequency
  • Prevents grease fallback, enhancing hygiene and fire protection

Honeycomb Filters

Designed with a precision honeycomb core, these filters break incoming air into thousands of mini streams for maximum grease separation and airflow efficiency.

Key Benefits
  • Lightweight and easy to handle, install, and clean
  • Optimised design increases grease capture and airflow
  • Perfect for high-use kitchens needing reliable filter performance
