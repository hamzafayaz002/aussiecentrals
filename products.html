Baffle Grease Filters

Baffle style filters are manufactured from premium stainless steel and function by forcing grease-laden air to quickly and repeatedly change direction as it rises through the filter. This advanced design drastically reduces fire risk by preventing dangerous combustible material buildup on the filter face.

Key Features & Benefits
  • Superior stainless steel construction for durability
  • 3x higher grease capture efficiency than standard baffle filters
  • Significantly reduces duct contamination and cleaning frequency
  • Prevents grease dropback onto cooking surfaces
  • Extends life of downstream filtration systems
  • Reduces fire risk through efficient grease separation
  • Easy maintenance with grease collection tray system

Honeycomb Grease Filters

The Honeycomb Grease Filter features innovative aluminium construction with a unique honeycomb design that splits incoming air into approximately 32,000 separate airstreams per square meter. This revolutionary design ensures superior grease separation and easy handling for commercial kitchen applications.

Technical Advantages
  • Lightweight aluminium construction for easy handling
  • 32,000 airstreams per m² for maximum grease separation
  • 50mm thick metal honeycomb media
  • Double compound curve design prevents nesting
  • Smooth walled honeycomb structure
  • Wide vertical handles for easy installation
  • Prevents grease entry into exhaust systems

Installation Guidelines & Care Instructions

Installation Requirements:
  • Filters must not exceed 30° angle from vertical
  • Handle sides must be in vertical plane
  • Ensure proper airflow direction
  • Secure mounting to prevent movement
Maintenance & Care:
  • Regular cleaning schedule recommended
  • Do not use caustic cleaners
  • Use approved cleaning solutions only
  • Inspect for damage during cleaning
Important: Proper installation and regular maintenance are crucial for optimal performance and safety compliance. Contact our professional team for installation assistance and maintenance schedules.
