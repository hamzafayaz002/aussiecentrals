Kitchen & Canopy Cleaning

What We Clean:
  • Exhaust hoods, filters, ductwork, and rooftop exhaust fans
  • Ovens, fryers, grills, cooktops, and all kitchen equipment
  • Stainless steel benches and prep areas
  • Splashbacks, tiled surfaces, sinks, and drains
  • Ceilings, lights, and floor areas within kitchen boundaries
  • Canopy inside and outside including duct fan cleaning
Why It Matters:
  • Reduces fire hazards
  • Ensures hygiene and safety compliance
  • Enhances ventilation and kitchen performance
Standards We Follow:
AS 1851-2012: Routine service of fire protection systems specific to kitchen exhausts
NFPA Guidelines: Ventilation system fire prevention
IKECA Principles: International Kitchen Exhaust Cleaning Association — international best practices
Certification: We provide validity certificates for canopy and exhaust fan cleaning, suitable for compliance and inspections.
